Elliott Nes

Los Angeles, CA



“With my paintings I explore the concepts of self developement, understanding that we are all in one form or another, coming together, or falling apart, often both at the same time.”




I have been cutting and painting stencils for 10 years. After years of changing styles, my paintings are made from sharp lines and broken images. Influenced by living in the city and interacting with people from all walks of life, I appreciate things that aren’t perfect but still hold together. Combined with my love of graffiti I strive to to reflect the beauty in the city’s dark and gritty corners - to me that dichotomy is what makes a place authentic.
